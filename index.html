BuildingOS

Reality Operating System for Infrastructure Governance

BuildingOS is an open infrastructure governance framework for connecting project intelligence, procedures, evidence, expert review, digital ledgers and continuous decision support across the full lifecycle of infrastructure projects.

One governance framework. Many infrastructure applications.

Core Modules

Project Intelligence

Discover and organise infrastructure project information from public and official sources.

Procedure Engine

Define stages, milestones, required documents, evidence gates and decision points.

Evidence Engine

Separate source documents, claim-level evidence and project profiles.

Review Engine

Record engineering, legal, finance, environmental and governance review.

Governance Engine

Connect evidence, procedure and expert review into structured go / no-go decisions.

Infrastructure Ledger

Maintain a traceable record of procedures, evidence, decisions and lifecycle events.

Positioning

BuildingOS is not a conventional BIM platform, project management app or consulting report system. It is a procedure-driven infrastructure governance framework. 

Workflow generates Evidence.
Evidence forms Ledger.
Ledger builds Trust.
Trust enables Investment.
Investment builds Infrastructure.
Infrastructure serves Civilization.
