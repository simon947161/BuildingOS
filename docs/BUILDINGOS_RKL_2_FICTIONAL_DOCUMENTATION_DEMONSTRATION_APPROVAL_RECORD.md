## Purpose

Record the Chief Architect decision authorising preparation of a bounded, documentation-only RKL-2 fictional demonstration brief.

This decision advances BuildingOS from the verified RKL-1 documentation gate into RKL-2 demonstration planning. It does not authorise implementation code or consequential regulatory use.

This decision authorises preparation of:

- `docs/BUILDINGOS_RKL_2_FICTIONAL_DOCUMENTATION_DEMONSTRATION_BRIEF.md`;
- a proposed fictional Markdown record-set structure;
- proposed fictional record templates and metadata fields;
- proposed Knowledge Garden indexes, links, backlinks, and maps of content;
- proposed documentation review, acceptance, stop, and supersession rules;
- proposed future gates for creating and reviewing a fictional demonstration record set.

The brief may cover fictional examples of:

- Australian NCC source metadata;

- jurisdictional variation records;

- project regulatory references;

- source-change notices;
- regulatory review queue items;
- attributed human-review records.

## Mandatory Demonstration Boundary

All future RKL-2 demonstration records must be unmistakably fictional and non-advisory.

Every proposed demonstration record must preserve an equivalent of:

```text
DEMONSTRATION_ONLY
FICTIONAL_RECORD
NO_LEGAL_EFFECT
NO_PROJECT_AUTHORITY
NOT_LEGAL_ADVICE
NOT_CERTIFICATION
HUMAN_REVIEW_REQUIRED
```

No fictional record may be written so that a reasonable reader could mistake it for an actual NCC, standard, NSW legislative, approval, certification, or project record.
