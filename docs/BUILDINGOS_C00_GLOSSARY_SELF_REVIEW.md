## Boundary Review

C00 preserves the following boundaries:

- Evidence is not fact by itself.
- Claim is not evidence.
- Review is not approval.
- Recommendation is not decision.
- Registration is not compliance.
- Ledger entry is not proof of correctness.
- Agency is not permission.
- Conformance is not legal, professional, financial, public, or operational
  approval.

## Risks Found

1. Some glossary terms may need tighter wording after architecture review.
2. C00 introduces ordering before C01-A, while older C01 planning records were
   written before C00 existed.
3. C00 should be frozen before later standards cite it as normative.

## Recommendation

Move C00 to Codex Engineering Review.

Do not proceed to C01-A until C00 is approved and frozen.
